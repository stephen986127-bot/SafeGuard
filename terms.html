Description of Service

SafeGuard is a personal safety app that monitors user activity and sends alerts to designated guardians when prolonged inactivity is detected. The app is designed for use cases such as:

  • People living alone who want a safety net
  • Elderly users whose family members want peace of mind
  • Individuals with health conditions who may need assistance
  • Travelers or outdoor adventurers wanting a check-in system

SafeGuard works by monitoring step count, location movement, and phone usage. If no activity is detected for the user's configured threshold, the app first notifies the user directly. If the user does not respond within the confirmation window, their designated guardians receive a push notification.
