How It Works

1

Set up your profile and add guardians

Sign in, configure your guarding hours and inactivity threshold, then invite your family or friends as guardians via QR code or search.

2

SafeGuard monitors your activity

The app checks for step count and phone usage every 30 minutes during your active hours. Normal activity resets the timer automatically.

3

You receive a check-in notification

If no activity is detected for your threshold period, SafeGuard asks "Are you okay?" — tap "I'm Safe" to confirm, or "I Need Help" for an emergency.

4

Guardians are notified if you don't respond

If you don't respond within the confirmation window, all your guardians receive a push notification to check on you.
